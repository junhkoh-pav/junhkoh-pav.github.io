Hanyang University


CV | Google Scholar | LinkedIn | Github


I am a Ph.D candidate at Hanyang University (HYU), advised by Professor Jun Won Choi.

My research interest lies in perception for autonomous vehicles and mobile robots. Particularly, I focus on 2D or 3D object detection, sensor fusion-based perception, object tracking, video object detection, and other related areas.

I am a recipient of Hyundai Motor Group Research Scholarship .
